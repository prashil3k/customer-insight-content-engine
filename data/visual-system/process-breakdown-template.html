How top sales teams build interactive demos that move deals forward — fast.

1
Capture your product as-is
Use Storylane's Chrome extension to record a live session of your product. No staging environment, no engineering help — just click through the flow you'd show a prospect.
~5 min
2
Clean up and focus the flow
Remove irrelevant screens, reorder steps, and blur sensitive data. Leave only the "aha moment" path your ideal buyer needs to see.
~10 min
3
Add guided tooltips and CTAs
Layer hotspots, step descriptions, and lead capture forms. Guide buyers through the value without a sales rep present — the demo sells itself.
~15 min
4
Personalize per account
Swap in the prospect's logo, company name, and use-case data. Personalized demos see 3× higher engagement than generic ones.
~2 min per account
5
Share, track, and follow up smart
Send a unique link. Watch who views which steps in real time. Follow up with context — "I saw you spent time on the analytics section…"
